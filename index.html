Frequently asked

How fast are match disputes resolved?

Admins review submitted footage within 24 hours. Verdicts are posted to both teams and the bracket is updated automatically.

Is there an entry fee for tournaments?

Qualifier rounds are free. Some Championship-tier brackets carry a buy-in, which is always shown on the tournament card before you register.

How is ELO calculated?

We use a modified Elo system weighted by round differential and opponent strength. Only sanctioned matches count toward your rating.

Can I transfer my slot to another clan?

Slot transfers are allowed up to 48 hours before the bracket locks. Contact support with both clan tags to request one.
